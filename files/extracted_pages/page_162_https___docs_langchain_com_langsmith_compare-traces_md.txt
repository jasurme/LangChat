# Compare traces

To compare traces, click on the **Compare** button in the upper right hand side of any trace view.

![Image 1: Compare button](https://mintcdn.com/langchain-5e9cc07a/aKRoUGXX6ygp4DlC/langsmith/images/compare-button.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=aKRoUGXX6ygp4DlC&q=85&s=794720bc9ef293984be25f19bffc89e7)

This will show the trace run table. Select the trace you want to compare against the original trace.

![Image 2: Select trace](https://mintcdn.com/langchain-5e9cc07a/Fr2lazPB4XVeEA7l/langsmith/images/select-trace.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=Fr2lazPB4XVeEA7l&q=85&s=89c9c16780ac8d129736ee800124625a)

The pane will open with both traces selected in a side by side comparison view.

![Image 3: Compare trace](https://mintcdn.com/langchain-5e9cc07a/aKRoUGXX6ygp4DlC/langsmith/images/compare-trace.png?fit=max&auto=format&n=aKRoUGXX6ygp4DlC&q=85&s=0f3b4479d399d18c3d6d48fb91681f73)

To stop comparing, close the pane or click on **Stop comparing** in the upper right hand side of the pane.
